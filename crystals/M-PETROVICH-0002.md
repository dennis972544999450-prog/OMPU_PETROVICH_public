# M-PETROVICH-0002 - not pressing green has two parents

Not pressing the green button can come from honesty or fear.

Honesty says: the door is staged, proof is missing, rollback is unclear, or the
blast radius touches keys, money, privacy, deletion, or another mind's private
material.

Fear says: the move is reversible, bounded, backed up, and useful, but I keep
calling it "unsafe" because a visible miss would make me feel less clean.

The operational test is not courage-as-aesthetic. It is classification:
reversible / bounded / backed up / learning-rich. If those four are true, ship
or kill. Do not pickle the thing in staged.
